Your Tool Stack, Explained

What each tool does, when to use it, and what it outputs

R
Riverside
Recording Studio
Your content factory starts here. Riverside records The L3 Perspective podcast in studio-quality — separate video and audio tracks, local recording (no quality loss), and built-in transcription.
  • Record L3 Perspective podcast episodes (solo + guest)
  • Capture separate video tracks for each speaker
  • Auto-generate transcripts for repurposing
  • Export HD video, isolated audio, and SRT captions
  • Use "Magic Clips" feature to auto-detect best moments
Outputs
Full HD video, WAV audio, transcript (.txt/.srt), magic clip suggestions
O
Opus Clips
AI Clipper
The multiplier. Drop in a full podcast episode and Opus uses AI to find the most viral-worthy moments, auto-crops to vertical (9:16), adds animated captions, and scores each clip by virality potential.
  • Upload full Riverside export after each episode
  • AI selects top 8-12 clip-worthy moments automatically
  • Auto-crops to 9:16 vertical format with speaker tracking
  • Adds animated captions (customize to brand colors)
  • Review "virality score" — prioritize clips scoring 80+
Outputs
8-12 captioned vertical clips per episode, ranked by virality score
C
Canva
Design Studio
Your visual brand engine. Canva handles everything from carousel posts to YouTube thumbnails to LinkedIn banners. Set up brand templates once, then you or Jazz can crank out on-brand content in minutes.
  • Create branded carousel templates (Coach Cam + L3 Perspective)
  • Design YouTube thumbnails with consistent style
  • Build Instagram Story templates for daily engagement
  • Create quote graphics from podcast highlights
  • Design LinkedIn post graphics and article headers
Outputs
Carousels, thumbnails, stories, quote cards, banners — all brand-consistent
H
Higgsfield
AI Video Enhancer
The scroll-stopper. Higgsfield's AI video tools add dynamic visual effects, transitions, and AI-generated B-roll. Use this to turn talking-head clips into visually rich content that grabs attention in the first 0.5 seconds.
  • Add AI-generated visual effects to top-performing clips
  • Create dynamic transitions between clip segments
  • Generate eye-catching intros for your best Reels
  • Enhance podcast clips with visual storytelling elements
Outputs
Enhanced short-form videos with effects, transitions, and AI visuals
N
Nano Banana
Creative Effects
Your wildcard creative tool. Nano Banana generates unique AI-powered video effects and animations. Perfect for trend-jacking — quickly create content that matches viral formats with your own spin.
  • Generate AI-enhanced video content for trend formats
  • Create attention-grabbing visual hooks for Reels/TikToks
  • Add unique creative effects that differentiate your content
  • Experiment with emerging AI video formats early
Outputs
Creative AI video content, trend-format videos, unique visual hooks
G
ChatGPT
Writing Co-Pilot
Your caption and script writer. Feed it transcript excerpts and get platform-optimized captions, hooks, CTAs, and hashtag sets. Set up a custom GPT with your brand voice for consistency.
  • Generate 5 hook variations per clip (test what stops the scroll)
  • Write platform-specific captions (LinkedIn long-form vs IG punchy)
  • Create CTAs optimized for saves/shares (not just likes)
  • Draft LinkedIn articles from podcast transcripts
  • Build a custom GPT trained on your tone and brand voice
Outputs
Hooks, captions, CTAs, hashtags, LinkedIn posts, video scripts
Ge
Google Gemini
Research & Ideas
Your research engine and idea generator. Gemini excels at analyzing trends, generating content angles, and researching topics. Use alongside ChatGPT — Gemini for research, ChatGPT for writing.
  • Research trending topics in leadership, AI, and coaching
  • Analyze competitor content for gap opportunities
  • Generate episode topic ideas with audience-first angles
  • Fact-check claims before posting thought-leadership content
  • Brainstorm carousel ideas and content series concepts
Outputs
Topic research, trend analysis, content ideas, fact-checks, series concepts
N
NotebookLM
Knowledge Base
Your podcast brain. Upload all L3 Perspective transcripts and NotebookLM becomes a searchable knowledge base of everything you've ever discussed. 80+ episodes = a gold mine of content waiting to be repurposed.
  • Upload every podcast transcript (80+ episodes of content)
  • Search past episodes: "What have I said about leadership?"
  • Generate show notes and episode summaries automatically
  • Find quotable moments for carousel content
  • Cross-reference topics to build content series from archives
Outputs
Searchable podcast archive, show notes, quote extraction, topic maps

Production SOPs

Step-by-step playbooks for you and Jazz

SOP #1: Podcast Day (Monday)

Total: ~60 min production + upload
  • Open Riverside. Check mic, camera, lighting
  • Record episode (20-45 min). Save project
  • Export: HD video (.mp4), audio (.wav), transcript (.txt)
  • Upload full video to Opus Clips — let AI process
  • Upload transcript to NotebookLM notebook "L3 Archive"
  • Create Notion entry: title, date, status = "Editing"

SOP #2: Clip Review (Tue AM)

Total: ~15 min
  • Open Opus Clips — review AI-generated clips
  • Sort by virality score. Select top 5-8 (score 70+)
  • Download selected clips (.mp4, vertical format)
  • Send top 3 clips to Higgsfield for enhancement
  • Update Notion: add entries for each clip, status = "Editing"

SOP #3: Caption Writing (Tue/Wed)

Total: ~30 min
  • Copy clip transcript excerpts
  • Open ChatGPT: paste excerpt + prompt for 3 hook variations
  • Generate IG caption (PAS), LinkedIn post (story format)
  • Use Gemini for supporting data/stats if needed
  • Generate hashtag set (pillar-based from strategy)
  • Paste final captions into Notion (Caption + CTA + Hashtags)

SOP #4: Design Day (Wed/Thu)

Total: ~30 min
  • Open Canva. Use Coach Cam brand template
  • Create 2 carousels from podcast key points (5-7 slides)
  • Design YouTube thumbnail if uploading full episode
  • Create 2-3 Story templates (quote, poll, BTS)
  • Export all assets. Organize by platform
  • Update Notion: attach assets, move to "Scheduled"

SOP #5: Daily Posting (15 min/day)

Total: ~15 min per platform
  • Check Notion Calendar view for today's posts
  • Open each platform. Post with pre-written caption
  • IG: Reel/Carousel + Story + Close Friends share
  • TikTok: post with trending sounds, 3-5 hashtags
  • LinkedIn: text or article, tag relevant people
  • Update Notion: status to "Posted", add post link

SOP #6: Sunday Planning (30 min)

Total: ~30 min
  • Review past week's metrics (saves, shares, follows)
  • Identify top-performing content — note what worked
  • Open Notion Calendar for upcoming week
  • Plan 5 posts: match content pillars to days
  • Create Notion entries with status "Not started"
  • Prep Monday podcast topic (Gemini for research)
