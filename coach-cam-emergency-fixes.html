Do these in order. Each one builds on the last. Total estimated time: 2-3 hours for all fixes.

FIX 1 — CRITICAL 15 minutes

Fix 3 Dead Links on forward-learn.com

60% of your website's social links send visitors to error pages. This is the #1 priority because it's costing you trust with everyone who visits your site — including potential enterprise clients.
  1. Log into your Wix dashboard (forward-learn.com appears to be on Wix)
  2. Navigate to your site's Header settings and find the Social Bar
  3. Instagram: Change the URL from:
    OLD: https://www.instagram.com/coach_cam_macias/ NEW: https://www.instagram.com/iamcmacias/
  4. TikTok: Change the URL from:
    OLD: https://www.tiktok.com/@coach_cam_macias NEW: https://www.tiktok.com/@iamcmacias
  5. Facebook: The page facebook.com/RCGROUPOPP is dead. Either remove the Facebook icon entirely, or create a new Facebook business page for Forward Learning Group and link to that.
  6. Now do the EXACT SAME changes in the Footer Social Bar (it has the same broken links)
  7. Publish the site changes
  8. Open your site in an incognito window and click every social icon to verify they all work
FIX 2 — CRITICAL 10 minutes

Update Your Instagram Bio (@iamcmacias)

Your Instagram bio says "Elevare Global" — a brand name that appears nowhere on your website, LinkedIn, or any other platform. Anyone who finds you on Instagram has zero way to connect you to Forward Learning Group or Coach Cam. Fix this immediately.
  1. Open Instagram → go to your profile → tap "Edit Profile"
  2. Change your Name from "Cameron Macias" to:
    Coach Cam | Leadership & Business
  3. Replace your Bio entirely with:
    CEO, Forward Learning Group ICF Credentialed Coach | Air Force Veteran Leadership, Business Optimization & AI Co-Host @thel3perspective Podcast forward-learn.com
  4. Update your Website link to forward-learn.com (or a Linktree that includes your key links)
  5. Change your Category to "Coach" or "Business Consultant"
Why this matters: "Elevare Global" in your bio creates a trust gap. If someone sees you on LinkedIn (Forward Learning Group), then checks your Instagram and sees "Elevare Global," they wonder: Is this the same person? Which company is real? That hesitation kills conversions.

FIX 5 — HIGH 20 minutes

Unify Your Profile Photos & Banners

Use the same or very similar headshot across every platform so people can visually recognize you instantly. Your LinkedIn headshot (professional, well-lit) should be your anchor photo.
  1. Take your best professional headshot (your LinkedIn photo is strong — use that or similar)
  2. Set this as your profile photo on: Instagram @iamcmacias, TikTok @iamcmacias, YouTube (if you have your own channel), and LinkedIn
  3. For banners/cover images, use a consistent template:
    Banner formula: "Coach Cam | [What You Do] | [Credibility]" Example: "Coach Cam | Leadership & Business Coach | ICF Certified | Air Force Veteran"
  4. Use the same color scheme across all banners (pull from your Forward Learning Group branding — red + dark tones work well with your existing website)

  3. YouTube L3 Perspective about: Verify instagram.com/thel3perspective link works ✓ — also add forward-learn.com as second link

FIX 7 — MEDIUM 45 minutes

Clean Up Your Instagram Grid (@iamcmacias)

Your grid currently has 1,599 posts with zero visual consistency: dark selfie videos next to nature photos next to challenge trackers next to cartoon avatars. No one can tell what your account is about by glancing at it.
  1. Archive (don't delete) old posts that don't match your coaching/business brand — personal photos, random challenges, lifestyle content that isn't on-brand. Archiving hides them from your grid without losing them.
  2. Aim to get your visible grid down to your best 30-50 posts that clearly show: you coaching/speaking, business insights (text posts or carousels), L3 Perspective podcast clips, client wins/testimonials
  3. Going forward, every post should fit one of these 4 content pillars:
    Pillar 1: Leadership Insights (tips, frameworks, lessons) Pillar 2: Behind-the-Scenes (your coaching process, events, clients) Pillar 3: Podcast Clips (L3 Perspective highlights) Pillar 4: Personal Story (veteran journey, why you coach, authenticity)
  4. Use a consistent filter/editing style for all photos — warm tones work well with your website's red accent branding
